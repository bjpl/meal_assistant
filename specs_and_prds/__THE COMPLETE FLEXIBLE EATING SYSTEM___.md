\#\#\# \*\*B. FRUIT PREPARATION TECHNIQUES\*\*

\#\#\#\# \*\*Caramelized Banana (5 minutes)\*\*  
\`\`\`  
INGREDIENTS:  
\- 1 banana, diagonal slices ½ inch  
\- 1 tsp butter  
\- Pinch cinnamon  
\- Optional: 1 tsp brown sugar

METHOD:  
1\. Heat butter medium pan  
2\. Add banana slices  
3\. Cook 2 minutes per side  
4\. Sprinkle cinnamon  
5\. Serve warm  
\`\`\`

\#\#\#\# \*\*Baked Apple (15 minutes)\*\*  
\`\`\`  
INGREDIENTS:  
\- 1 apple, cored (leave bottom)  
\- 1 tsp butter  
\- 1 tsp cinnamon  
\- Optional: 1 tsp honey

METHOD:  
1\. Core apple leaving ½ inch bottom  
2\. Fill cavity with butter, cinnamon  
3\. Microwave 3 min OR  
4\. Oven 375°F for 15 min  
5\. Rest 2 minutes before serving  
\`\`\`

\#\#\#\# \*\*Grilled Pineapple (5 minutes)\*\*  
\`\`\`  
INGREDIENTS:  
\- Pineapple, ½ inch rings  
\- Optional: lime juice

METHOD:  
1\. Heat grill pan high  
2\. No oil needed  
3\. Grill 2 min per side  
4\. Char marks \= ready  
5\. Optional lime squeeze  
\`\`\`

\- Add 100-cal planned snack

\- Switch to grazing pattern  
\- Check water intake  
\`\`\`

\`\`\`
